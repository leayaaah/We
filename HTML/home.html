Đà Lạt

Da Lat is a beautiful city located in the Central Highlands of Vietnam, known for its stunning landscapes, cool climate, and vibrant flower gardens. Visitors can explore the picturesque valleys, lush pine forests, and numerous waterfalls that make this city a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. The city is also famous for its colonial architecture, charming cafes, and vibrant markets, offering a unique blend of cultural experiences. Whether you're looking to relax in the serene environment or engage in outdoor activities like hiking and biking, Da Lat has something to offer for everyone. Don't miss the chance to visit the iconic Crazy House, a whimsical architectural marvel that attracts visitors from all over the world.

Cần Giờ

Can Gio is a coastal district located in the southern part of Vietnam, known for its rich biodiversity and unique mangrove ecosystems. This area is a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve, offering visitors a chance to explore its lush landscapes, diverse wildlife, and tranquil beaches. Popular activities in Cần Giờ include boat tours through the mangroves, bird watching, and visiting the local fishing villages to experience the traditional way of life. The district is also home to several historical sites, including the Ho Chi Minh City Museum of History and the Cần Giờ War History Museum, which provide insight into the region's past.
